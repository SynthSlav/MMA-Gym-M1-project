Range of classes

Boxing Classes

Boxing is a timeless sport that combines skill, endurance, and discipline. It's not just about throwing punches—it's about mastering footwork, building mental toughness, and developing overall physical fitness. Whether you're looking to improve your cardio, learn self-defense, or compete, boxing is a journey that challenges and transforms you.

Muay Thai Classes

Thai boxing, or Muay Thai, is known as the 'Art of Eight Limbs' for its use of punches, kicks, elbows, and knees. This dynamic martial art builds strength, agility, and resilience while teaching practical self-defense skills. Whether you're training for fitness, competition, or personal growth, Muay Thai offers an empowering journey that pushes your limits and sharpens both body and mind

Gi BJJ

Gi Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) focuses on technique, leverage, and strategy, using the traditional gi uniform to execute grips, chokes, and submissions. It’s a martial art that emphasizes control and precision, making it ideal for practitioners of all sizes and strengths. Gi BJJ not only hones your physical skills but also sharpens your mental focus, teaching discipline and resilience on and off the mats.

No-Gi BJJ

No-Gi Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a fast-paced, dynamic form of grappling that eliminates the traditional gi uniform, focusing on grips and submissions using athletic wear like rash guards and shorts. Without the use of the gi, the techniques rely more on body control, speed, and fluidity, making it ideal for those interested in MMA or self-defense. No-Gi BJJ offers a modern twist on classic BJJ, emphasizing adaptability and practical application in real-world scenarios.

Kids-class

Our kids' classes provide a fun and safe environment for children to learn martial arts while developing important life skills. Through age-appropriate training in boxing, BJJ, and other disciplines, kids build confidence, discipline, focus, and respect. These classes not only improve physical fitness but also teach valuable lessons in teamwork and perseverance, all while ensuring they have fun and stay engaged.

Women only class

Our women's classes are designed to empower, inspire, and build confidence through martial arts training. Whether it's boxing, BJJ, or self-defense, we focus on creating a supportive and motivating environment where women of all levels can challenge themselves, get fit, and learn valuable self-defense skills. These classes promote strength, resilience, and community, offering an inclusive space for women to grow both physically and mentally.

MMA/Striking class

MMA and Striking classes combine the best elements of boxing, Muay Thai, kickboxing, and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu to develop well-rounded fighters. These sessions focus on mastering stand-up techniques like punches, kicks, and knee strikes while integrating key grappling skills for self-defense and competition. Whether you're training for a fight or looking to improve your fitness and confidence, MMA and Striking classes push you to new heights in skill, strength, and endurance.

S & C class

Strength and Conditioning (S&C) classes are designed to enhance overall athletic performance by improving strength, endurance, flexibility, and mobility. Tailored to complement your martial arts training, these classes focus on functional movements that help you build a strong foundation for any sport. Whether you're looking to boost your fitness, prevent injuries, or take your performance to the next level, S&C classes provide the tools and techniques needed for sustained improvement.
